## 🎮 **How to Use**

### 🎤 **Voice Recognition:**

1. **Select your language** from the language buttons
2. **Click the microphone** button to start listening
3. **Speak clearly** about your health concerns
4. **Watch the conversation** appear in real-time
5. **Get intelligent responses** from the AI assistant

### 💬 **Sample Conversations:**

#### **English:**

- **You:** "Hi, I have a fever"
- **Assistant:** "I understand you're experiencing fever. Can you tell me more about your symptoms? How long have you had the fever and what's your temperature?"

#### **Hindi:**

- **You:** "नमस्ते, मुझे सिरदर्द है"
- **Assistant:** "Headaches can have various causes. Can you describe the type of pain and how long you've been experiencing it?"

#### **Emergency:**

- **You:** "Emergency" or "आपातकाल"
- **Assistant:** "This sounds urgent. For immediate medical emergency, please call 108. Would you like me to help you call emergency services?"
